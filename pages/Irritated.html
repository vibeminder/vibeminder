😒 What's Got You So Irritated?

Feeling a bit snippy lately? Could be from any number of things - nagging responsibilities, annoying people, or just a general sense of unease. Whatever's rubbing you the wrong way, don't let it fester.

Coping Techniques to Try 😌

  • ☕Take a break and treat yourself with a warm drink
  • 🗣️Talk it out with a friend who can lend an understanding ear
  • 📚Channel that irritation into a productive activity or hobby

    • Hang in there, you've got this! 💪
